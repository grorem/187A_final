Types

Wine is composed of different types and varieties, ranging from red to sparkling wine, with each embodying their own unique flavors. This page contains all of the information you need to understand these differences.

This site provides an excellent visual guide that makes it easy to select a wine on a spectrum of dry to sweet, high to low tannins (red), crisp to high intensity (white).

This site provides a brief introduction to those who would like to learn more about popular varieties of red and white wines. It is especially helpful in providing descriptions on how the each particular wine tastes.
