About Wine
Wine is an alcoholic drink, often made with fermented grapes, but can be made using other types of fruits. It has one of the longest and richest histories dating as far back to Mesopotamia to modern times where it is often complementing a meal. In the following sublinks, readers will explore history of wine, popular wine varieties (and color) and the health benefits of wine. Readers can also then follow up on current wine news and blogs and learn the process of making wine at home.
Wine Basics
A foundation of wine basics can be appreciated by the beginner and learned wine sommeliers and connosieurs alike. The following topics will introduce red and white wines (and their varieties), and wine etiquette at social events. Taste and appreciation are discussed by examining a wine drinker's various sensations and palate. Finally, we include information to locate local and regional vineyards.
Recommendations
Wine enthusiasts are always interested in wine pairing with different types of food. Many of them are often interested in cooking with wine to enhance flavors, such as marinading meat. Because beginners are reluctant to spend on pricier wine, this site will provide in good starting point by introducing reliable bottles at budget-friendly prices. In addition, readers can check out recommendations given for special celebrations and holidays.
Supplies
For the wine collector, it is crucial to look into equipment geared towards serving and long-term storage of wine. Wine that is properly stored ages better, and will keep its freshness. This page highlights decanters and aerators, storage, and glassware. In addition, this page will also serve those who are interested in the equipment to begin winemaking at home.
