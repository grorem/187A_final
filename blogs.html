Blogs & News

The topic of wine, like many other topics, is not static. This page contains wine blogs and news to help wine drinkers stay updated on the current events related to wine and new wine releases.
vinography.com
This site provide news updates with topics of interest globally, such as from China, Hungary, France, and the Americas.
vintagecellars.com
This informative site provides up-to-date wine and storage topics. A helpful archive is listed on the left side for specific topics.
gayot.com
This is a useful site that archives new wine releases by year and then by month in descending order. Global wines are featured starting within the Americas, Australia, Africa and extending into Europe and Asia.
decanter.com
This is a premier site for all wine news, featuring daily updates.
