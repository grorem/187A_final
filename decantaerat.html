Decanters and Aerators

ADecanters and aerators are small handheld storage containers to aerate wine. These devices would infuse air with oxygen, and is used alternatively to swirling a wine-glass. Some wine decanters are also used to separate sediments to smoothen out the texture of a wine. Simply put, decanters and aerators filter wine, in effect enhancing its flavors.
