Cooking

Similar to food pairing with wine, wine is often used in cooking to enhance the food's texture. This page contains links that will teach you how to integrate wine into your cooking.
whatscookingamerica.net
This informative site explains how wine should be integrated into the cooking process. Its cooking formulas and alcoholic content information are especially useful for those who want to cook with wine.
winesecrets.org
This is a detailed guide on making wine sauce. It provides many suggestions on making different variations of wine sauces.
allrecipes.com
This is a great online resource to find recipes that integrate wine as an ingredient. The cooking directions are easy to follow and many recipes have helpful ratings and reviews given by those who have tried the recipes.
