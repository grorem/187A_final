Food Pairing

A specific type of food can be paired with a particular wine to enhance the dining experience. As such, food pairing is both an art and an enjoyment. This page contains links that will teach you the basics of food pairing and provide you with food pairing recommendations.
Food and Wine Pairing: Matching Food to Wine
This simple and interactive wine pairing guide makes the process of pairing food to wine more enjoyable. You can choose a food type using the given picture options on the top navigation, and then select a more specific food type on the left side of the screen for a recommended wine and a brief explanation.
Food and Wine Pairing: Matching Wine to Food
This different interactive guide follows a different approach to wine pairing, in which you choose from a wine type rather than from a food type. After choosing from the wine type on the left navigation, a recommendation of some food matches in addition to some simple explanations will appear on the right side of the screen.
Wine Mag: Food Pairing Essentials
This is a good introductory guide to understanding how to balance elements of food and wine to create wonderful dishes. The writer provides a list of food and wine pairings to complement their explanations.
Matching Food and Wine: Food Pairing Articles
This is an informative website to learn more about wine pairing. You can find articles using four categories that expand the readers' wine pairing techniques, but should be aware of its search engine as it often leads to no results.
