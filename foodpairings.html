Food Pairing

A specific type of food can be paired with a particular wine to enhance the dining experience. As such, food pairing is both an art and an enjoyment. This page contains links that will teach you the basics of food pairing and provide you with food pairing recommendations.
foodandwinepairing.org
This simple and interactive wine pairing guide makes the process of pairing wine with food more enjoyable. Readers can choose the food type using the given picture options on the top navigation, and then select a more specific food type on the left side for a recommended wine.
matchingfoodandwine.com
This is an informative website to learn more about wine pairing. Readers can find articles using four categories that expand the readers' wine pairing techniques, but should be aware of its search engine as it often leads to no results.
winemag.com
This is a good introductory guide to understanding how to balance elements of food and wine to create wonderful dishes. The writer provides a list of food and wine pairings to complement their explanations.
